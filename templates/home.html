Welcome to this project! On this platform we want to collect data, in order to understand human opinion in relation to posts generated autonomously by an intelligent algorithm. We're going to present you with some posts, and ask you to interact with them in the way that feels right to you.




About this work

This platform is part of an academic project, taking place at the University of Minho - Portugal, whose purpose is to obtain an algorithm capable of generating Posts (messages that are published on a website) awakening Feelings and interacting with the Personality of users. The use of social networking and online communication has been growing exponentially over the past few years. In 2021, 56.8% of the world's population has a profile in at least one social media. Consequently, the spread of fake news had a proportional growth. Thus, there was a need to validate information that appears online, which promoted the emergence of agencies that are dedicated exclusively to this task. However, a post referring to a credible fact-validation entity does not guarantee its reading. More is needed. The reality is that most of the time this type of publication is overlooked and the information it contains, sometimes valuable, escapes the user. It is essential to change this trend. In this project, it will be used the introduction of feelings in the Posts and the analysis of the readers personality, in order to arouse interest, promote interaction and a greater acceptance of the published content. This platform, is intended to be the starting point for this study, allowing to collect data, enabling the identification of trends regarding the decisions of users of a social media when confronted with a given publication.
