About this work

This platform is part of an academic project, taking place at the University of Minho - Portugal

The research hypothesis that motivated this work was to test whether posts designed with emotion and personality increase their engagement.

This environment is designed to simulate the interface of a social network microblog and allow the collection of data to understand human opinion on posts made autonomously. Another goal is to gain insights into how information is disseminated on Twitter. The data collected will allow us to answer the following questions:

    Is it possible to find a pattern for how a tweet goes viral?

    What factors influence how Twitter users interact with a tweet?
